The program "myls.cpp" simulates the command ls provided in shell
The options implemented are 
-a ,
-l , 
-h , 
-A , 
-R , 
-s , 
-S ,
-t , 
-d .

The options can be given together same as in a normal ls command or one option at a time
eg: ./myls -al
    ./myls -alR 
    ./myls -l 
    ./myls -­R  -­lt
    ./myls  ­-l <file/directory>
    ./myls  ­-l -­t <file/directory> 
    ./myls  ­-lt > output.txt etc.
